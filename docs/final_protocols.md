Protocol: Post-Entry Information Restriction
Common Behaviour removed: Monitoring additional information after entry
Rule
After entering a trade, no chart lower than the entry timeframe may be viewed.
Duration
Applies until 5 consecutive trading days are completed without violation.
Failure
Viewing any lower timeframe chart after entry ends the run.

Rule

Duration
Applies until 5 consecutive trading days are completed without violation.
Failure
